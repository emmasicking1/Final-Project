Project Overview

This project explores the history of women’s athletics at Mount St. Joseph University, focusing on early development, the impact of Title IX, and the trailblazers who built the foundation for women’s sports today.

Thesis

Women’s athletics at MSJ grew from limited opportunities into a respected and competitive program due to the leadership of early trailblazers and the evolving impact of Title IX.

Why This Matters Today

The opportunities female athletes have today are rooted in the work of those who came before them. Understanding this history highlights the importance of equality and continued growth in athletics.
