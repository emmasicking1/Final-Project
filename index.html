Project Overview

This project explores the early history of women’s athletics at Mount St. Joseph University, focusing on the development of sports programs, the impact of Title IX, and the trailblazers who built the foundation for women’s athletics today.

Thesis

Women’s athletics at MSJ grew from limited opportunities into a respected and competitive program due to the leadership of early trailblazers and the transformative impact of Title IX.

Why This Matters Today

The success of women’s athletics at MSJ today is built on the efforts of early athletes and coaches who created opportunities where none existed. Their legacy continues to shape the experience of student-athletes today.
