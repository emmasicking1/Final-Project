Project Overview

This project explores the history of women’s athletics at Mount St. Joseph University, focusing on early development, the impact of Title IX, and the trailblazers who shaped the program.

Thesis

Women’s athletics at MSJ evolved from limited opportunities into a more equitable program due to the impact of Title IX and the efforts of trailblazing athletes.

Why This Matters Today

This history helps explain the opportunities female athletes have today and highlights the importance of continued progress in equality.
